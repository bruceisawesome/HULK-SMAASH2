About Hulk:

Hulk (a.k.a. Bruce Banner) is an absolute behemoth made to smash. He is an unstoppable BULKY and MUSCULAR monster fit for any challenge. He gains most of his satisfaction from complete and utter destruction. However, that is not his motto, he dedicates himself to saving lives from villians, even if it costs him his life. Hulk may look frightening, but he is here to rescue and be a do his duty as an actual superhero.

Hulk's Characteristics:

  1. His ROARS! will shake any of his demons to their core
  2. His TOWERING size will DWARF everything around him
  3. His GREEN appearance warns others that he will not think twice before striking
